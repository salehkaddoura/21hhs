Physical Therapy (PT) Helps Regain Function

Whether it was an accident or a result of injury or disease, the role of a physical therapist is to help our members return to maximal function. Physical therapists also work with individuals to prevent loss of mobility by developing fitness- and wellness-oriented programs for healthier and more active lifestyles. Our physical therapists recondition, retrain, and strengthen your motor skills in wheelchair accessibility, pool therapy, stretching programs, pain management, joint motion, and various forms of tissue healing. In addition we provide services dealing with weight management, diabetes and maintaining regular exercise routines.

Physical therapy may be applicable for someone experiencing:

  • A loss of balance
  • A fall
  • Diffucult walking
  • Joint pain
  • Back pain
  • Hip or Knee Replacement Surgery
  • A stroke
  • Heart Failure
  • A heart attack
  • Any noticeable decline in function
